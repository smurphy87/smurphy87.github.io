      • 1976-77 Montreal Canadiens

      This team was simply dominant, with nine of its players eventually being inducted into the hockey hall of fame. They held an astounding 60-8-12 record to their name with the likes of Guy Lafleur, Larry Robinson, and Jacques Lemiare leading the way. They also had hall of fame goalie Ken Dryden in net and Scotty Bowman, perhaps the best coach in NHL history, behind the bench. The only lost two games through the entire playoffs and won the cup with a four game sweep of the Boston Bruins.
